About us!

Welcome to Theo Coffee!

Founded in 1999, Theo Coffee has been serving exceptional coffee in the heart of Zamalek, Cairo. Our journey began with a simple passion: to create a space where coffee lovers could enjoy premium brews and unwind in a warm, inviting atmosphere. Over the years, we’ve grown into a beloved local spot, known for our rich coffee blends, artisanal pastries, and commitment to quality. Whether you're stopping by for a quick espresso or spending a leisurely afternoon with friends, Theo Coffee offers the perfect space to savor the finest coffee in Cairo. Join us and be a part of our story!
